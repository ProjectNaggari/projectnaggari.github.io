In this page, you can see the data from the inventory book concerning the printed titles in Naggari's possession at the moment of his death. The inventory-maker only noted the value, a short title, that it is printed, and whether it is mīrī (government-printing) or barrānī (non-government printing). To help students, we also present in this first set of data the translitareted short title from the inventory. If you click on the transliterated title you can find the actual print, if we could identify, of which one copy was in al-Naggari's possession. And we added 50 more metadata categories based on this identified copy. If you want to learn more about our working method, levels of certainty, and about our categories, read the Reflections section.
